The Lion Who Learned to Be Gentle
A Story About Kindness
"A big lion learns that a gentle heart brings the most joy to all his friends."
⭐ Meet Leo the lion, a big and strong lion who loves to play but sometimes forgets his strength.

Leo's Big Roar

Leo was a big lion. He loved to run. He loved to play. But Leo had a very loud roar! His paws were big and strong. Leo sometimes forgot his strength. He just wanted to have fun.

"Let's play!" Leo would shout. His loud voice made small animals jump. They would run away fast.

Pip Hides Fast

One sunny day, Leo saw Pip the rabbit. Pip was eating a green clover. Leo wanted to play with Pip. He took a big, happy jump. "Hello, Pip!" Leo roared loudly.

Pip made a small squeak. He hopped away very fast. Pip hid behind a big rock. His tiny heart beat quickly. Leo felt a bit sad. All the other small friends also hid.

❓ Quick Check!

How did Pip feel when Leo roared loudly?

3 / 7
Leo Feels Sad

Leo watched Pip shake behind the rock. He saw Pip's soft ears droop down. Leo looked at his own big paws. He saw the empty spot where Pip had been. A sad feeling filled Leo's chest.

"Oh no," Leo whispered. "My loud play scares everyone." He knew his strength was a problem.

"I will be gentle."
🦁

A Gentle Try

Leo made a good choice. He decided to try something new. He walked very slowly to the rock. His big paws made soft, quiet sounds. Then, Leo made a tiny, quiet purr. It was a sweet sound.

"Pip," Leo whispered gently. "I am sorry. I want to play nicely." Pip peeked out.

"Quiet helps friends feel safe."

5 / 7
Happy New Play

Pip slowly hopped closer. Leo lay down very carefully. He pushed a soft leaf with his nose. Pip giggled and ate the leaf. They played a quiet, fun game. Leo felt happy in a new way.

It felt good to make Pip feel safe. He liked being gentle.

"Kindness makes everyone happy."
